S-Cream is safe for most healthy adult women. Our formula has been dermatologically tested and is made with ingredients that have been clinically studied. However, we recommend caution if you:

  • Are pregnant or breastfeeding
  • Have sensitive skin or known allergies to any ingredients
  • Have any medical conditions affecting your intimate areas
  • Are currently using other topical products in the same area

We always recommend doing a small patch test on your inner wrist before applying to intimate areas. If you experience any discomfort, rinse immediately with cool water.

For optimal results with S-Cream:

  1. Timing: Apply 15-20 minutes before intimate activity
  2. Amount: Use a small pea-sized amount (a little goes a long way!)
  3. Application: Gently apply to external intimate areas, focusing on the most sensitive regions
  4. Absorption: Allow the cream to fully absorb - it leaves no residue

The cooling sensation upon application is normal and will transition to enhanced sensitivity. Remember that S-Cream enhances your natural responses, so combining with foreplay and relaxation leads to the best experience.
